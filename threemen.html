Three Men in a Boat

“What the eye does not see, the stomach does not get upset over” ― Jerome K. Jerome


The story of Three Men in a Boat revolves round, more or less, the adventure of three men who decide to take a break from their monotonous life and take a boating vacation up the river Thames. The story is narrated by a certain Englishman named J. who embarks upon the journey along with his friends George and William Samuel Harris.
